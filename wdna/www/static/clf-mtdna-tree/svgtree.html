Phylogenetic tree of complete mitochondrial genome sequences of Canis lupus familiaris reconstructed with maximum parismony method. Haplogroup names are in red. The mutations that are haplogroup diagnostic are listed above the haplogroup name. The mutations are transitions, unless suffix (A, C, T or G) that represents the transversion is added. Insertions are marked by an “.” following the number and inserted nucleotides and deletions are marked by “del” following the position of deleted nucleotide. Names of haplotypes are GenBank accession numbers (colour correspond to specyfic geographical origin)."
